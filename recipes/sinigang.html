Sinigang na Baboy

Description

The sourness of this homemade Sinigang would surely make you crave for more!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1/2 kilo of pork
  • Aromatics:
    • 1 minced onion
    • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
    • Crushed black pepper
  • Tomatoes: 2 tomatoes, diced
  • Vegetables:
    • 1 bundle of kangkong
    • 1 radish, sliced
    • Green chili peppers
  • Others:
    • Pork Knorr cubes
    • Sinigang mix
    • 5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Gather/Prepare all ingredients.
  2. Fry the pork until brown.
  3. In the same pan, saute garlic, onion, tomatoes, and finally put the pork. Season with black pepper and cook until the aromatics stick in the pork.
  4. Add 2 cups of water, pork knorr cubes, and sinigang mix. Let it simmer. Stir occassionally.
  5. Put all of the vegetables and add more water to taste. Cook until vegetables are soft.
