Tinolang Manok

Description

This classic Filipino dish would introduce you best to the world of Filipino dishes.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1/2 kilo of chicken
  • Aromatics:
    • 1 sliced onion
    • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
    • Black pepper
  • Vegetables:
    • Tanglad
    • Malunggay leaves
    • 1 sliced Sayote
    • Sili leaves
  • Others:
    • 1 chicken Knorr cube
    • 5 cups of water

Steps

  1. Gather/Prepare all ingredients.
  2. Saute garlic, onion, and finally put the chicken. Season with black pepper.
  3. Add 2 cups of water, chicken knorr cubes, and tanglad. Let it simmer. Stir occassionally.
  4. Put the rest of the vegetables and add more water to taste. Cook until vegetables are soft.
